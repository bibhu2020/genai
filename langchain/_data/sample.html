Introduction to AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming how we live, work, and interact with the world.

Applications of AI

AI is being used in various industries including healthcare, finance, education, and entertainment.

Healthcare

AI helps in diagnostics, patient monitoring, and drug discovery.

Finance

Fraud detection and algorithmic trading are popular applications in finance.

Challenges and Ethics

There are growing concerns around AI fairness, transparency, and accountability.

Future of AI

The future of AI looks promising but requires responsible development and deployment.
